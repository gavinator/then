# Vision

## Mission

In a world awash with news and information about the present, there's something about the past or future, including the distant past or future, that makes us wonder: What has been said, or what will be said, then? What stories were never told, or never will be?

## What success looks like

The app combines two modes of interaction and engagement:

- A daily-return habit app (like a horoscope or Wordle) — people check in for one dispatch a day
- A narrative/worldbuilding tool people use to generate consistent alternate histories or futures over time

Some users will be passive consumers, while others may be more active participants in generating these alternate histories or futures. Users may be both at different times.

## Audience

This app is for sci-fi/history enthusiasts, people who like ambient/atmospheric apps, and general curiosity-driven casual users.

## Non-goals

This app is explicitly *not* trying to be a real news aggregator, a factual historical archive, or a chatbot/assistant.
