# Overview

Status: ready to build

## Purpose

The splash screen provides a moment to introduce the brand and tone while giving the app time to initialize.

## Layout

"Then." displays in large serif font at center middle. Below, "News from whenever" tagline displays in smaller font.

## Interaction

N/A

## Animation

A small ribbon that tapers to 0% opacity on either side displays.

"Then." originates at center middle at 0% opacity, utilizing a bloom effect as it goes to 100% opacity.

Concurrently, tagline "News from whenever" appears below, in situ, going from 0% to 100% opacity.

## Accessibility

Text can be read aloud by a screen reader. Text is not selectable.

## Acceptance Criteria

On load, ribbon displays, followed by "Then." and "News from whenever".
